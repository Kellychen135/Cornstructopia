What is Constructopia
In Constructopia, you, the player, are a construction worker. As a construction worker, you build little homes for your little village. Working, however, tires and stresses you out. In order to make sure you stay healthy, you must complete actions that correspond to what the issues are having at the moment.

Activities to be completed based on health:
sad > go home and spend time with family/go to park
tired > sleep
hungry > grocery store > choose healthy food
thirsty > drink water
weak > exercise
dirty > wash up
